"B") ~ "Excellent" )) print(academic_risk_flag) ``` ##8 STUDENT WHO IMPROVED IN THE EXAM ```{r} improved_students <- df %>% filter(Exam_Score > Assignment_Score) print(improved_student) ``` ##9
